Additionally, The Child Tax Credit reduces your tax bill up to $1,000 per child every year until your child turns 16. Single taxpayers who work and families who have two earners who file jointly can also claim the Child and Dependent Care Credit. This can pay up to 35 percent of the first $3,000 you spend on child-care expenses for one child. Or the first $6,000 for expenses for two or more children.
