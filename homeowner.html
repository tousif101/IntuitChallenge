An important factor in buying a home is the down payment which is NOT tax-deductible item. However, if you cash out a retirement plan to fund your down payment, let the plan administrator know that this is the purpose of the distribution. You can avoid the early withdrawal penalty if under age 59 1/2 if you are a first time home buyer and the funds are used for acquisition, which includes buying, building or rebuilding a first home. Closing costs are not deductible as well. However, there are some deductible items such as property taxes and loan origination fees.
